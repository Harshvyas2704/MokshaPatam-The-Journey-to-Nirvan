# App Icon Brief — Mokshapat

A self-contained brief for generating the app icon. Hand this whole file to an image-generation AI (or a designer). A ready-to-paste prompt is at the bottom.

---

## 1. The App in One Line

**Mokshapat — Journey to Moksh**: a premium, contemplative digital recreation of the ancient Indian spiritual board game (the philosophical ancestor of Snakes & Ladders), depicting the soul's 285-square journey from the human realm to liberation (Moksha).

## 2. Mood & Feeling

The icon must feel **sacred, calm, premium, timeless, and spiritual** — like a sacred emblem or a detail from an illuminated manuscript / temple.

It must **NOT** feel: arcade-like, casino-like, cartoonish, neon, loud, gamified, or like a generic board-game app.

## 3. Visual Theme

Traditional Indian Spiritual Heritage. Inspiration:

- Temple architecture & sacred geometry (mandala, yantra)
- Ancient manuscripts, palm-leaf texts, gold-leaf illumination
- The "soul" as a glowing orb / sacred flame
- Restraint and symmetry — centered, balanced, dignified

## 4. Color Palette (exact, from the app theme)

| Role                | Hex        |
| ------------------- | ---------- |
| Saffron             | `#E58A2B`  |
| Deep maroon         | `#5C1A1B`  |
| Muted gold          | `#C9A227`  |
| Copper              | `#B06A3B`  |
| Ivory               | `#F4ECD8`  |
| Deep canvas (bg)    | `#1C0F0A`  |
| Liberation glow     | `#E6C25A`  |
| Soul orb core       | `#E6F2FF`  |
| Soul orb aura       | `#4FA3FF`  |

**Recommended icon background:** a warm, deep maroon→near-black radial (`#5C1A1B` → `#1C0F0A`), so gold/saffron motifs and the luminous soul read with reverence. Gold (`#C9A227`) and ivory (`#F4ECD8`) are the primary "ink" colors.

## 5. Core Motifs (pick / combine)

In rough priority order:

1. **The soul orb** — a small luminous blue-white sphere with a soft aura (core `#E6F2FF`, halo `#4FA3FF`), the hero of the app. Reads as a "spark of consciousness."
2. **The path to Moksha** — a single sacred ladder or upward path/spiral rising toward a radiant point of light at the top (liberation glow `#E6C25A`).
3. **Sacred geometry frame** — a subtle gold mandala / lotus / yantra ring enclosing the motif.
4. **Om / lotus** — an understated `ॐ` or lotus as a secondary accent (optional; don't clutter).

> Strong single concept beats a busy collage. The icon is tiny on a home screen — favor **one bold, centered, symbolic idea**.

## 6. Concept Directions (any one works)

- **A:** A glowing soul-orb ascending a slender gold ladder toward a burst of liberation light, on deep maroon, ringed by a faint gold mandala.
- **B:** A centered gold lotus/mandala with a single luminous orb at its heart and a radiant halo above — pure, emblematic, minimal.
- **C:** A stylized vertical sacred path/spiral from a dark base (earthly) to a bright gold apex (Moksha), with the soul orb partway up.

## 7. Style Rules

- Flat-to-soft-gradient, elegant line work; gold-leaf / manuscript texture is welcome but keep it subtle.
- High contrast and legible at **small sizes** (test mentally at 48×48 px). No fine text, no thin details that vanish when shrunk.
- Centered, symmetrical, with comfortable padding from the edges.
- No literal dice, no cartoon snakes, no English/Sanskrit lettering as the focal element (an emblem, not a wordmark).

## 8. Technical Output Specs

Produce a **square** master with **no transparency**, centered with safe margins.

- **Master:** 1024×1024 px PNG (sRGB), full-bleed background (no rounded corners — the OS masks them).
- **iOS:** single 1024×1024 App Store icon; **no alpha / transparency**.
- **Android adaptive icon:** deliver as two layers if possible —
  - _foreground_ (the emblem, transparent PNG, centered within the inner ~66% "safe zone"),
  - _background_ (the solid/gradient maroon canvas).
  - Keep all critical content inside the central circle (Android masks to circle/squircle/etc.).
- Also nice to have: a simplified 1-color / monochrome version for notification/tinted contexts.
